Find your GP to view your record

You can view your record online if:

  • your GP is part of the pilot scheme
  • you have registered for your GP's online services
  • your GP has granted you permission to view your record
How to sign up for online services

Ask your surgery for a form to join online services. You might have to provide photo identification and proof of address. After you fill in the form the surgery will send you the information you need to sign in.

Search by surgery name or GP name to go to your booking system.
