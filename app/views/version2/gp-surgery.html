What patients say about this surgery

Every year, the GP Patient Survey gives patients a chance to say how they feel about their GP surgery.

The survey is independent and asks people about their experiences of:

  • booking an appointment
  • waiting times
  • staff at the surgery

You can see results for this surgery on the GP Patient Survey website.
