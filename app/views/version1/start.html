Apply to register with kjk

This application is made up of two parts.

Part 1 Your details

You’ll be asked for information about yourself including:

  • the GP you’re currently registered with if you have one
  • the name and address under which you’re registered with your current GP (if they’re different)
  • your NHS number if you know it

    An NHS number is 10 digits, for example 943 476 5919

    You can find your NHS number:

    • online
    • on a printed prescription
    • on a letter from your GP or a hospital
    • on your NHS medical card if you have one

This is to help {{ practiceName }} find your medical records.

Part 2 About your health

You’ll be asked questions about any allergies, medication and your general health.

This is to help {{ practiceName }} understand your needs.
