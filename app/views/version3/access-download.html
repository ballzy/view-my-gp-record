Goals of the project

The NHS is busy designing new digital services to help you take control of your own health and care needs to ease the pressure on our frontline services.

GP online services already offer you the ability to do certain transactions online, including book GP appointments, order repeat prescriptions and access information in your GP record.

Currently you have multiple ways to access these online services. We are testing whether patients feel it would be easier and more straight forward to access all these services in one place.

Therefore we aim to transform the patient journey by enabling you to stay on NHS.UK to access your online services. By doing this, we hope to improve your experience and gain valuable insight in several key areas.

  • The value and impact of the NHS brand.
  • The barriers to entry and use of the new process to resolve these.
  • The ways in which different features on the product are used.

We hope to use these findings to help us improve the existing service.

What did we do?

We designed a GP online service to be piloted on NHS.UK, with the focus on providing you with the ability to access to your GP Record, book an appointment, and order repeat prescriptions.

This pilot is now live in a small cohort of GP Practices around the country. It will improve our understanding of your needs and help us assess your experience of our services, in order to meet those needs.

During the pilot stage, we’ll be testing and checking your responses and experience in accessing your GP record, and other online services on the NHS.UK page. We’ll use these insights to improve the usability and trust in these services.

The future

Once we’ve analysed the answers to the key areas described previously, we’ll then focus on incorporating the successful elements into the future design of any new service. We then expect to share the learning from the project with a wider audience, including providing any relevant research to the current providers of the service. In this way you’ll be able to see the changes you requested and understand how they’ve been delivered.
