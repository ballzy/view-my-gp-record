Register as a patient

This surgery is accepting new patients

You need to go to the surgery to register. Take with you:

  • proof of identity (for example your passport)
  • proof of your current address (for example a gas or electricity bill)
  • your last GP surgery’s name and address
  • your NHS number (you can find this on any document you’ve received from the NHS)

You’ll also need to fill in a registration form. You can download and fill in a form before going to the surgery. But the receptionist might have other forms that you need to fill in.
