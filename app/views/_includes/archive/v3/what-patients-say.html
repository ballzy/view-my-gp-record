What patients say about this surgery

The GP Patient Survey gives patients a chance to say how they feel about their GP. In the most recent survey:

  • 59% of patients would recommend this GP surgery
  • 78% describe their overall experience at this surgery as good
  • 70% describe their experience of making an appointment as ‘good’
  • 80% say the last appointment they got was convenient
