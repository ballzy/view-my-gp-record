Services and clinics

Child immunisation

Child vaccinations up to 5 years old.

You can book an appointment for this clinic without seeing a GP.

Asthma clinic

See a practice nurse for regular check-ups and advice on lifestyle, inhaler techniques and medication.

You can book an appointment for this clinic without seeing a GP.

Diabetes clinic

See a practice nurse for advice on lifestyle and management of diabetes.

You can book an appointment without seeing a GP.

Minor surgery clinic

We can carry out minor surgery, for example removal of moles and skin lesions.

A GP needs to refer you to this service.

Primary care counselling service.

Book an appointment to see a counsellor or psychiatric nurse, or to be referred to local psychiatric services.

You can book an appointment without seeing a GP.

Stop smoking clinic

See a nurse who specialises in helping people who want to stop smoking.

You can book an appointment without seeing a GP.

Travel vaccinations

We give vaccinations to children and adults.

You can book an appointment without seeing a GP.
